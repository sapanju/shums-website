Shums Kassam

CTO of Hatchways

Shums is the co-founder and CTO of Hatchways, a startup focused on universalizing the ‘co-op’ education model beyond post-secondary students. He co- founded his first company – a mobile shopping app called Blynkstyle – during his undergrad, and it was acquired by Kik in 2015. He spent two years at Kik, where he led the server development team in Toronto and managed Kik’s developer platform.
